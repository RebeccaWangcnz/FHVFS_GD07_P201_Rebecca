1. Wall System: Walls have 2 states: blue and white. Player can pass the blue wall, but can't pass the white wall.
2. Shooting System: Players can use WASD to shoot in 4 direction to change the wall states.
3. Health System: Players have health which means they can be attacked for 3 times.
4. Upgrade System: Each time after collecting 5 pills, there will be a new enemy instantiate at a random location.
